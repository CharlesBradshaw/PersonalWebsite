Why I Code

I have a passion for solving problems by creating software that interacts with the world around me. This passion drives me to be the best I can be, and has kept me exploring the changes in technology.

It all started at age seven when my brother taught me how to make a simple quiz in Bash. Since then I have continued to challenge myself to learn more and more. Most recently I have immersed myself in machine learning. It is a very difficult topic, but what keeps me pushing forward is knowing I will have the ability to change the world if I can use machine learning in a creative way.

Professional Bio

I have 2.5 years of professional experience. I primarily use Java, Python, and MySQL, but I have experience with Ruby, JavaScript, PHP, and Cassandra. Over my career, I have developed sofware including a checkout processing system, queries to automatically detect at risk clients, a data scraper for thousands of cars, and a multi-regional database using Cassandra and AWS.
